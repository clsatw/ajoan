 view_agenda Objective and purpose of the projects

To implement home automation & surveilance at low cost & low power consumption

To help the environment by saving energy

The chip can be programmed like any other Arduino devices, which is very convenient for me to buid home automation/surveilance. It is low power-comsumption, low cost, and supports sleeping mode, which is a good choice for my projects.

info_outline Approach to the problem

Iot question 101: How to access the device from anywhere?

It is easy to connect esp8266 devcies to the net; however, there are some problems need to be resolved:

  • When programming, we usually hard code ssid and password into the devie for wifi connection, but what if we take it to somewhere else or we change our router's ssid or password?
  • After the device get connected to the Net, it is assigned a dynamce private ip adress by DHCP server and doesn't have a DNS name, something likes www.abc.com. How can we acess it from anywhere?
  • How to trigger an event for the device to take action(s)? e.g, I want the device to automatcially turn on/off aquarium waterpump at a specified time.
  • Last but not least, we need to put the device into sleeping mode to save power.

Something to the rescue

After researching and pulling most my hair out, the follwoing services/framework are adpoted to achieve the goal:

  • 3rd party wifi manager library
  • MQTT (Message Queuing Telemetry Transport)
  • IFTTT (If This Then That)
  • interrupt and deep_sleep mode in arduion
